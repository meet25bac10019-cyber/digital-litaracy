SOCIAL MEDIA DO’S AND DON’TS CHECKLIST (COLLEGE STUDENTS)

DO’S: 
1. Think before you post.
2. Protect your privacy by using strong passwords and privacy settings. 
3. Be respectful and professional in your interactions. 
4. Verify information before sharing it. 
5. Use social media for networking and building connections.

DON’TS: 
1. Do not share personal or sensitive information. 
2. Do not post inappropriate or offensive content. 
3. Do not engage in online arguments or cyberbullying. 
4. Do not overshare about school or work. 
5. Do not assume posts are temporary.
